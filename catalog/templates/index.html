The Colonial Heritage Foundation (the Foundation) is a 501(c)(3) corporation dedicated to the preservation o the values, culture, skills and history of America's founding. To accomplish this mission, the Foundation engage in a broad array of activities. Among these are the development and presentation of educational exhibits, the coordination of reading and discussion groups to encourage the study of America's historical writings, the presentation of lectures and seminars regarding America's founding era, the coordination of historical reenactments and skill demonstrations, and the coordination of internships and apprenticeships that teach the occupational skills of early America.
